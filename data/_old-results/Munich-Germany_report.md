### Technical University of Munich
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **University Fees**:
  - **Annual Fee**: $182 USD
  - **Details**: The Master's program in Quantum Science & Technology at the Technical University of Munich has no tuition fees for international students from non-EU countries. Mandatory costs include a semester fee of approximately $91 USD per semester, covering student services and a public transportation ticket, totaling about $182 USD annually. Scholarships and financial aids are available.

- **Cost of Living**:
  - **Annual Living Cost**: $35,000 - $45,000 USD
  - **Details**: Minimum living expenses are estimated between $18,000 and $22,000 USD annually, covering accommodation, food, transportation, and personal expenses.
